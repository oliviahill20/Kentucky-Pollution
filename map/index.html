U.S Tornado Paths from 1950-2019

University of Kentucky Geography

Tornado paths from 1950-2019 extracted from the National Weather Service published by the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Adminstration. (weather.gov) Tilesets extracted from Mapbox Stuido ((Mapbox.com))

Tools used in this project

  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled. If the map got popular it would require a fee to use.
  • Mapbox and Stuido and Windows 10 were used to make this map.

    • Recent Events

  • I live in Bowling Green, Ky and in wake of the recent tornado I have created a map showing the tornado paths throughout the U.S from 1950-2019. Though this recent tornado is not on this map, the area that is zoomed is my neighborhood - one of the more heavily hit areas in the city. Many of the buildings outlined in this area of the map are no longer standing. If you zoom out on the map, you will notice that there hasn't been a tornado come through this area anytime after 1950. I have also included images to the right of the US 31 Bypass in the past as well as the present.
